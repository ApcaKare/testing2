As covid raged on and infected more and more people around me my cough had gotten just worse. Been a few days since I could taste food properly and blamed it on my cold. ... As days progressed, I got more suspicious and got myself tested. As I feared, I was tested positive. Life at the quarantine blocks was grim so I decided it was time to loosen up a bit and finally get to know a few people around me. Turns out that makes my symptoms only worse, and decided I better stay by myself. Several people around were showing symptoms of fever soon enough. It was a rough time, but thankfully no deaths occured. After quarantining for two weeks I was finally let out, breathing in the fresh air and grateful for the life we get to live, regardless of the circumstances.

Amulya
